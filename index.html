A dimension of sound. A dimension of another dimension.

I like to make weird noises using computers. I make tools so that you can do it too.

Audio Plugins

  • Empy (2023)

    Fine-tune the sound of lossy compression, for a variety of distorted sounds.

  • Fish (2023)

    Give your tune a sub-aquatic stink, using the guts of a real MP3 encoder. Easy to use! Just one knob!

  • Envelope Follower (2022)

    Use the amplitude envelope of a track to control any knob of any plugin, using MIDI CC messages.

  • Splutter (2021)

    Spiraling, chaotic pitch shifting delays. Spooky, bubbling diminished chords. Laser-gun sounds. Ominous growling. Sparkling overtones. Another plug-in that does that? Yawn.
